Welcome to the Editorial New Hotel, a young, cool and chic hotel for type lovers. This type specimen for Editorial New takes the form of a narrow, vertical booklet inspired by the typeface’s condensed letterforms. Conceptualised as a hotel experience, the specimen demonstrates the font’s versatility across different applications – from wayfinding to body copy. The design juxtaposes modern elements (such as the bold “brat green” color) with traditional layouts to reflect Editorial New’s character as a contemporary serif with vintage charm. Through interactive materials and careful typography, the specimen showcases the typeface both as a functional communication tool and compositional element.
