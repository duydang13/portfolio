CHECK YOUR MAILBOX.

Speculative Design/Design Fiction
Editorial Design
Branding/ Packaging Design

My capstone project, “Check your mailbox” is a speculative design project set in 2047, exploring a dystopian future where AI automation has created permanent mass unemployment under unchecked capitalism. Through three mailbox diegetic artefacts – a government aid package, an independent newspaper, and junk mails – the project materialises this future through comprehensive graphic design works that showcases my skills in editorial design, packaging, and branding. Furthermore, this project demonstrates my ability to merge conceptual thinking with polished execution while tackling complex socio-economic themes. It represents my growing interest in using design as a tool for social commentary, as well as future scenario and world building.
