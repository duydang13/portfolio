MOOD - MILK BRANDING

Brand Positioning/ Brand Strategy
Visual Identity
Packaging Design

Mood is a flavoured milk brand designed for adults aged 25-35, bringing a playful and witty twist to a traditionally youthful product. Developed through extensive market research, competitor analysis, and focus group interviews, the brand fills a unique niche with its guilt-free, dairy-only offerings. Mood’s visual identity is distinctive, featuring hand-drawn graphic elements, a friendly mascot named Timoothy, and expressive "Moody faces" on its packaging. A custom secondary typeface complements the clean serif logo and sans-serif primary typography, adding a personal touch.
