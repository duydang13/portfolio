MELTDOWN FESTIVAL

Visual Identity/ Branding
Print/ Digital Collateral Design

This project reimagines the branding for the 30th anniversary of Meltdown, the UK’s longest-running artist-curated music festival, with a theme of blending nostalgia and innovation. The visual identity pays homage to past editions while embracing a forward-looking aesthetic. A fusion of classic serif and modern sans-serif typefaces, combined with a grid-based yet dynamic layout, reflects the interplay of tradition and avant-garde. The logo highlights the number 30, symbolising continuity and progression. Four posters reinterpret past festival editions, maintaining a unified system for the anniversary while celebrating each curator’s unique influence. The identity seamlessly adapts across media, ensuring a cohesive and contemporary festival experience.
