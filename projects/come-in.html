COME IN WE ARE OPEN!

Visual Experimentation
Zine Design

“Come In, We Are Open!” investigates the dynamic interplay between the colours of natural and artificial foods, presented through a zine designed to mimic a department store catalogue. Each spread juxtaposes organic fruits and vegetables with their processed counterparts, exploring complementary colour palettes that emphasise the vivid contrasts between the two. This exploration of colour delves into its psychological impact, revealing how bold, artificial hues captivate consumer attention, yet natural food colours can be equally vibrant and evocative. The project examines the cultural and emotional resonance of color in shaping perceptions of food, questioning the relationship between visual appeal and nutritional value.
